I make original data-driven visualizations. Using my background in scientific research and policy analysis, I aim to create socially just narratives, help inform decision making, and increase the accessibility of data. Currently, I am finishing up my MS in Data Visualization at Parsons School of Design in NYC. If you like my work and want to collaborate, feel free to contact me .
